About this project

In the course Design and Analysis of Software Systems Spring 2025 at IIIT-H, our first assignment was to make a buy/sell listing website for IIIT. I may have gotten slightly overboard with the design though, and added dynamic listing creation, seller reviews and ratings, image zoom on hover, share buttons, skeleton loaders, and so on. Which explains why I got full marks for this, not to brag or anything though. In fact the skeleton loaders were the first time I did something like that, so I may or may not have added a fake delay on some pages just so people can enjoy their beauty. And there's an AI chatbot powered by Claude, that may not work because I do not have the API key set anymore. The site was designed using ShadCN, another first. I also decided for a pirate theme because I liked the idea.
